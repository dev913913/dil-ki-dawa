# ❤️ Dil Ki Dawa — Medicine for the Heart

Dil Ki Dawa is a web app that suggests **Hindi / classical songs** based on your mood or what you are feeling.

Instead of searching for songs, you simply express your emotions — and the app finds a song for you.

---

## 🌐 Live Demo
https://dil-ki-dawa.vercel.app

---

## 💡 Concept

> हर एहसास का एक गाना होता है।  
> Every feeling has a song.

This app is designed as a small emotional companion that connects feelings with music.

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

- Select a mood  
- Or write what you are feeling  
- Click **Find My Song**  
- Get:
  - Song suggestion  
  - Singer / Film / Year  
  - A short reason why it fits  
  - YouTube playback  

---

## ✨ Features

- Mood-based selection  
- Free text input  
- AI-based song suggestions  
- Explanation for each song  
- YouTube player  
- “Try another song” option to get a fresh match  
- Save favorite songs locally in the browser  
- Better mood detection using mood chips + text cues  
- Clean and aesthetic UI
